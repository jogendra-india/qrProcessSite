System Overview
  • BHEL QR-based Employee Entry/Exit System (BHELQEEE) is developed in lieu of the third party supplied biometric-based attendance system to save huge expenditure being incurred on the annual maintenance of these systems.
  • The in-house developed system can cater to the end-to-end attendance requirement of any BHEL office/sites/units across India.
  • A unique QR code is created for each employee, which can be downloaded from ESS and used in the form of a hard copy or in the mobile phone for daily scanning on BHELQEEE system.
  • A centralized application is created on ESS for employees usage where they can see their attendance and get the absences regularized from their controlling officers.
  • The system is already in operation at various units like Corporate R&D-Hyderabad, Corp. Office Siri Fort-Delhi, Noida Township, PEM Noida, ASSCP Gurgaon, CLD Noida, 06 RODs i.e. Chandigarh, Chennai, Mumbai, Kolkata, Jaipur and Lucknow. It has a proven track record of reliable operation and processed more than 20 Lakhs QR scans till now.
